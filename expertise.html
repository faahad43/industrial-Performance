    • LE DIAGNOSTIC
    EN PERFORMANCE INDUSTRIELLE POUR PRENDRE DE LA HAUTEUR

    Before any action plan, it is of course necessary to draw up a complete and detailed inventory of your situation, to be able to identify the levers for optimizing your industrial performance.

    The challenges to be met are often numerous and avenues for improvement must be analyzed one after the other and prioritized in industrial performance to gain momentum.

    To do this, we spend time with you in the field, listening to you (management, management, staff) and observing your operating methods , to identify the causes of waste and non-performance and then propose an adapted operational action plan. . Discover our other areas of expertise and services: The Quick win site ; operational coaching ; TRS ; continuous improvement ..

    EXCHANGES AND OBSERVATION
    FOR A COHERENT ACTION PLAN

    Object

    Define the purpose of the diagnosis and the need defined by the site management/shareholders.

    Recuiel

    Organization chart + Distribution of staff by workshop.
    Number of staff (permanent, fixed-term and temporary) / Absenteeism rate.
    Opening hours of the production unit / Staff working hours.
    List of factory indicators (productivity - OEE - MOD - cost price - etc.).
    Quantities produced per day by family of finished products over the last 12 months. Number of actual labor hours per day over the last 12 months and per workshop if possible (with Holder and CDI breakdown).
    Number of finished product references + Distribution of sales by product family over the last 12 months.

    Description

    Physical Flow + Information Flow.

    Diagramme Spaghetti

    Main workshop/factory flows (man/PM/waste/…).
    Walking forward.
    Crossing of flows.

    Identification

    By field observation: With instantaneous observation.
    In a working group: With POST-IT use.
    Classification of waste by theme.
    Participation in on-site meetings
    .

    Interview

    Production manager + Maintenance manager. Supervision of operational production and maintenance.
    Sector operators.
    Example of questions to ask: seniority, development, career path, mode of operation, role, performance management, Lean knowledge, communication, main problems encountered.

    Recommendation ET Estimations

    From the waste noted during the diagnosis.
    Productivity.
    TRS.
    Reduction of losses.
    MOD gain, etc.

    Proposal

    Breaking down the project into major stages.
    Setting the number of days per step.
    Provisional schedule.
    Integration of CoPil into the proposal.
    Construction of a retro-planning.

    Proposition

    Duration of the mission.
    Cost of the mission.
    Travelling expenses.
    Billing and payment terms.
    Method of payment of professional expenses.
    Deadline and method of payment.
